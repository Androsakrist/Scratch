Who is He?
THE INHERITANCE CYCLE WRITER

Christopher Paolini is one of the most well known American authors. He was born on 17th of November, 1983 in Los Angeles. His best piece of work is termed as Inheritance Cycle, which comprises of publications like Inheritance, Eldest, Eragon and Brisingr. He produced his first manuscript at Paradise Valley, Montana, where he initially used to live.

In the year 2005, the sequel of Eragon was made public with the title, Eldest. Brisingr was the third publication of the Inheritance Cycle and was released in the year 2008. Paolini planned the Inheritance Cycle to be completed in three publications, but few details of Brisingr were left and then published afterwards in the last book of the series named as Inheritance.
